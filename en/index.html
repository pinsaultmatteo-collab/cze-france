00The company

Your industrial partner in the automotive sector.

With many years of expertise in the automotive industry, CZE France designs the most efficient, high-performing and durable solutions for its customers. We understand the sector's challenges and meet them with equipment designed for the field.

Our range covers the entire vehicle life cycle: high-density storage with our cantilever racks, precise dismantling with our hydraulic shears, and copper recovery with our separators. Three concrete levers to gain space, time and value.

Our commitment to quality, innovation and service is uncompromising. We don't want to be just a supplier, but a true partner in your profitability.
