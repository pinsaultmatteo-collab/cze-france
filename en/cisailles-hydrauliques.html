Boost your productivity with our dismantling shears

Hydraulic dismantling shears play a crucial role in many applications, particularly for ELV centres, scrapyards and rescue services. These specialised tools are essential for the efficient, safe handling of end-of-life vehicles as well as for post-accident rescue operations.

For ELV centres and scrapyards

The shears allow safe, efficient and environmentally responsible deconstruction of end-of-life vehicles. Designed to cut precisely through metals and other materials, they ease disassembly and improve the separation of the various materials that make up vehicles, supporting recycling and reuse in compliance with environmental regulations.

For rescue services

In accidents involving heavily damaged vehicles, these shears let rescue teams cut through bodywork quickly and safely to free trapped occupants. Their ability to cut the toughest metals can make the difference during critical extrication operations.

Combining power, precision and reliability, these tools are a valuable investment for any dismantling or rescue professional.
