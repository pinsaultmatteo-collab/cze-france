What is cantilever racking?

Cantilever racks are industrial shelving designed to store heavy, bulky items efficiently and safely. They consist of horizontal beams called "arms" attached to vertical uprights. The items to be stored rest on these arms, giving easy access to each one. The arms can be adjusted in height to suit different load sizes.

The benefits of cantilever racking

  • They maximise storage space by using all the available height.
  • They are easy to install and use day to day.
  • They are rugged and durable, built from premium-grade materials.
  • They are endlessly expandable, for total flexibility at any time.

How to assemble a CX cantilever rack?

The CX cantilever is delivered as a kit and is fully bolted: no welding required. Assembly follows a few simple steps, detailed in the assembly guide:

  • Bases & uprights — the upright sits in the welded base and is fixed with M20×40 bolts (recommended torque 417–500 N·m).
  • Bracing — the X and H braces mount on the uprights with M12 bolts; their number and position depend on the cantilever height (from H10' to H28').
  • Floor anchoring — the bases are levelled then anchored to the floor with M16×100 anchors, for full stability.
  • Fitting the arms — each arm is fixed to the upright with Ø29×140 safety pins and adjusts in height to suit your load plan.
  • Stop pins (optional) — fitted at the arm ends to secure loads against any forward slipping.

The full guide details every step, the tightening torques and the bracing positions for the chosen height.
